Who is Rachel Noe?

Rachel Noe was born in the USA on 8 June 1993 – her zodiac sign is Gemini and she holds American nationality. She is a model and an Instagram star, probably best known for her Instagram account as there are currently over 100,000 people following her, while she has uploaded around 1,600 pictures onto it, mostly taken during her everyday life, plus others taken during her photoshoots.

Early life, parents and education

Rachel was raised alongside her younger sister Gracie Noe in the US, by her father who was an accountant, and her mother who worked as a clerk in a lawyer’s office. Rachel became interested in modelling while attending elementary school as she started reading girly magazines with her peers, while her teachers and parents kept telling her about how beautiful she was, and that she could become a model one day.

She became physically active when at high school, as she wanted to stay fit and shape her body – she played soccer and tennis, and was eventually noticed by a modelling scout who invited her to her first photoshoot.

Having already launched her modelling career, Rachel didn’t attend college after matriculating in 2011.

Career as a model and social media star

Rachel initially made money by advertising companies on websites such as Union Bay, and she soon became a fashion and beauty blogger before becoming a full-time model.

Everything changed for her after she was signed to Heffner Management in February 2013, and around the same time started uploading pictures onto her Instagram account. Since then she has posed for various clothing brands and companies, with one of the most popular among these being Nordstrom Inc., and has been featured in numerous magazines, including “Prairie Underground Magazine”.

Rachel is active on various other social media networks, and is very popular on the short-video sharing service TikTok, as her account is currently followed by over 420,000 people, and has gathered around nine million ‘likes’ of all her videos combined. The website is mostly used for uploading funny videos, which is what Rachel does and is considered to be hilarious which is how she has gathered that many fans – she and her cat have become internet sensations.

She was also once active on her Twitter account, which she launched in November 2011, but abandoned it in 2015, so slowly lost her followers and there are only 78 remaining today.

As of August 2020, Rachel is in a relationship with Vinnie Hacker, she hasn’t married and doesn’t have children.

Rachel likes to travel and has been to many US states such as Florida, California and New York, and has also vacationed in several European countries, including Greece, Croatia and Italy.
