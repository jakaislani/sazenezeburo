Alexis Sanchez’s Nationality

Where is Alexis Sanchez from?

Alexis Sanchez’s Education

After Alexis was adopted by his uncle Jose, he enrolled him in a training academy due to the boy’s impeccable talents. Even though he wasn’t earning much Jose still assisted the boy. 

Alexis worked hard, did odd jobs and at a point had to beg to feed himself. This was how low the footballer got. The football director of ‘Cobreloa football Club’ helped turn his life around. Due to this kind gesture of his, Alexis worked hard not to disappoint him.

Alexis Sanchez’s Career

He became the youngest player in “Copa Libertadores” to play in the local tournament, aside from that he was offered a one-season contract in 2006.

In December 2006, he won his first title and led the team to win 4- 0. He did impressive with a great performance for his national team in the ‘Under- 20 FIFA World Cup’. In 2008, he Join ‘Udinese’

In 2011, Barcelona had a deal with Udinese for Alexis to join their team but instead made the deal with ‘Real Madrid’. His initial seasons were difficult times I tell you but in January 2014, he scored against ‘’Elche’ leading them to victory. it was too late, cause ‘Barcelona’ had already given up.

 On August 10, 2014, he was purchased by ‘Arsenal’ matched against ‘Manchester’ and led them to a win 3 –0.

The year 2015 – 2016 proved to be ineffective years of his career but in the year 2016- 2017 he won the “Player of the Season Award’ by scoring the opening goal for ‘FA Cup final win against ‘Chelsea’.  He was then purchased by ‘Manchester United’ and became the first Chilean player to join the team.

Alexis Sanchez was selected to receive the ‘Golden Ball” award, which is mostly awarded to the best player of the tournament

Alexis Sanchez’s family & siblings

Why is Alexis Sanchez famous?

When the footballer’s father abandoned them, they had only their mother but fortunately for them Jose Martinez, adopted Alexis which reduced the burden of Martina Sanchez (Alexis’s Mother). He worked hard to impress his family’s rescuer which paid off when he began his career as a footballer from 2005 to 2006 for Corbello where he led his club to victory in 2007. The young footballer’s talents and hard work were discovered and praised by the Chileans. He won the Copa American Golden Ball Award. His talents gave him the name “wonder boy” which also led him to the top, making his family proud. He makes an estimated amount of $500,000 per week. His people made a statue in his honor.
