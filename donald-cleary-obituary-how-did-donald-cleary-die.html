Donald Cleary Obituary And Death Cause

Donald Cleary, 79, passed away at Morton Hospital on August 27 surrounded by loving family and the kind care of hospice staff.

Donald was born to the late Harold J. Cleary and Elizabeth (Donovan) Cleary on February 24, 1944, in Boston, Massachusetts.

He attended St. Mary’s Elementary and Coyle High School while living in Taunton and graduated with the Class of 1962.

He went on to acquire a bachelor’s degree in teaching from Providence College. He went on to get an MA in 1974 and a CAGS in 1980, all from Northeastern University.

In 1967, he wed Judy Conway, and the couple eventually had four children: Donald Jr., Jeffrey, Jessica, and Keith.
