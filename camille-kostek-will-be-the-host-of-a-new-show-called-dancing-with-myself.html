Camille Kostek Will Be The Host Of A New Show Called Dancing With Myself

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Dancing With Myself, NBC’s new reality dance competition will begin on Tuesday, May 31, 2022, at 10 p.m. ET. The program will pit ordinary people against each other in a series of dance challenges devised and performed by celebrity creators Shakira, Nick Jonas, Liza Koshy, and presenter Camille Kostek. Each week, famous experts will execute these tasks designed exclusively for the program, which the competitors must complete. The contestants will have a limited amount of time to learn the dance steps and perform in front of the judges and the live audience, both of whom will play a role in determining who advances.

According to the official summary of Dancing With Myself, the format is as follows:

“As the competition goes through the rounds, the creators on the judging panel offer quick input and choose their favorites to advance, along with audience selections. However, the studio audience ultimately determines who is declared the greatest dancer of the night and receives the $10,000 award.”

Who Is Camille Kostek?

Camille Veronica Kostek was born on February 19, 1992, in Killingworth, Connecticut to contractor father Alan Kostek and gym manager mother Christina. The Polish, Irish, and Jamaican-born American model, actress, and TV personality have four younger siblings.

Camille took ballet training at an early age and went on to participate in various national contests while also studying the art form at the Broadway Dance Center in New York City. She was also a member of Eastern Connecticut State University’s dance team, anchor of its network program, cheerleader, and lacrosse varsity captain.

At the age of 19, she began professional cheering with the Hartford Colonials of the United Football League before moving on to the NFL as a cheerleader for the New England Patriots.
