Why Mahabaleshwar Strawberry is famous?

Mahabaleshwar has the perfect cool and dry weather conditions with temperature at 18 °C to 25 °C and fertile red soil rich in iron that is conducive for growing strawberries and accounts for about 85 percent of India's strawberry production. The area produces about 15,000 tons of fruit each year.
