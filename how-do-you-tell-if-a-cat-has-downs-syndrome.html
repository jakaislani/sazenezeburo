How do you tell if a cat has Down's syndrome?

September 2024 · 6 minute read

A so-called "Down syndrome cat" typically manifests some distinctive characteristics, including:

  • Broad noses.
  • Upturned eyes (which may be set widely apart)
  • Small or unusual ears.
  • Low muscle tone.
  • Difficulty walking.
  • Difficulty with elimination (urination or defecation)
  • Hearing or vision loss.
  • Heart problems.
